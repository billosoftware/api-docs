## User Experience Focus

This update brings several user-facing improvements that enhance your daily experience with Billo Invoicing. We've focused on better notifications, improved interface consistency, and polished details that make the platform more professional and user-friendly.

- Enhanced email notification system for better client tracking
- New invoice history export feature for tax returns
- Improved invoice table display with timezone consistency
- Marketing site polish and brand clarity improvements
- Security details and FAQ content enhancements

## New Features

### Invoice History Export

A brand new page dedicated to helping you with your tax returns by providing easy access to your invoice history.

- Complete invoice history export functionality
- Pre-filled date ranges for common tax periods
- Custom date range selection for specific needs
- CSV download option for external record keeping
- On-page viewing for quick reference
- Streamlined tax return preparation process

### Enhanced Email Notifications

We've improved our notification system to give you better visibility into client interactions whilst respecting your preferences.

- Invoice viewed email notifications - receive alerts when clients view your invoices
- Payment receipt emails now respect your notification preferences
- One-time notifications per invoice to avoid spam
- Granular control over which notifications you receive

### Improved Interface Consistency

Better data display and interface consistency across the platform for a more professional experience.

- Invoice table dates now display consistently in your time-zone
- Improved draft table functionality with proper profile integration
- Better date formatting across all invoice-related tables
- Consistent time-zone handling throughout the application

### Marketing Site Enhancements

We've polished our marketing site to better communicate Billo Invoicing's value proposition and professional appearance.

- Enhanced "Look professional" benefit card with before/after progress bars
- Clearer value comparison for potential users
- Improved visual hierarchy and content presentation
- Better conversion-focused design elements

## Improvements

### Brand Consistency

We've made Billo Invoicing's branding more consistent across all touchpoints for better recognition and professionalism.

- Consistent "Billo Invoicing" branding in browser tab titles
- Updated Apple web app title for better clarity
- Improved brand presence across all user-facing elements
- Standardised naming conventions throughout the platform

### Content & Security

Enhanced content presentation and security information for better user understanding and trust.

- Fixed FAQ line breaks for proper answer rendering
- Clarified security details including AES256-GCM encryption
- Improved content readability and structure
- Better information hierarchy in help sections
