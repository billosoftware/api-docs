## Introduction

We're excited to announce Billo Invoicing Version 1. A full-featured invoice management platform built for modern businesses. This release includes everything you need to create, send, track, and get paid for your invoices.

Billo Invoicing helps businesses and freelancers streamline invoicing, manage clients, process payments, and stay compliant. After months of development, testing, and feedback from our early users, we're proud to share what makes Billo Invoicing special.

## Core Features

### Invoice Management

Create and manage invoices with ease. Billo Invoicing gives you complete control over your invoicing workflow:

- **Customisable line items**: Add products, services, and descriptions with flexible pricing
- **Multiple invoice templates**: Choose from Default, Professional, Minimalist, or Modern designs
- **Reusable templates**: Save your invoices as templates for faster future creation
- **Draft mode**: Work on invoices without sending them until they're ready
- **Custom invoice numbering**: Set your own numbering scheme
- **Multi-currency support**: Default GBP with support for multiple currencies
- **Notes and payment instructions**: Add context and payment details
- **Status tracking**: Track invoices through their lifecycle: Draft â†’ Sent â†’ Viewed â†’ Paid

### Recurring Invoices

Automate your recurring billing:

- Set up recurring invoices (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly)
- Automatic generation via scheduled jobs
- Auto-send option for hands-off invoicing
- Never miss a billing cycle

### Invoice Sharing & Delivery

Share invoices securely and professionally:

- **Secure token-based public preview**: Share invoices without exposing sensitive data
- **PDF download**: Professional PDF generation for all invoices
- **Email delivery**: Send invoices directly to clients
- **View tracking**: Know when clients have viewed your invoices
- **Notifications**: Get alerted when invoices are viewed or paid

## Payment Processing

Get paid faster with integrated payment processing powered by Stripe.

### Stripe Integration

- **Stripe Connect OAuth**: One-click account connection, no API keys needed
- **Payment links for invoices**: Automatic payment link generation
- **Standalone payment links**: Create payment links independent of invoices
- **Automatic payment reconciliation**: Payments automatically update invoice status
- **Payment receipt emails**: Clients receive receipts automatically

### Payment Tracking

- Mark invoices as paid manually or automatically
- Track payment dates and methods
- Complete payment history
- Receipt generation for all payments

## Client Management

Keep all your client information organised in one place.

### Client Database

- **Comprehensive client profiles**: Contact information, company details, and tax IDs
- **Address management**: Store clients address
- **Payment terms per client**: Set custom payment terms
- **Preferred payment methods**: Track how each client prefers to pay
- **Notes and tags**: Organise and categorise clients

### Bulk Operations

- **CSV client import**: Import multiple clients at once
- **Template CSV download**: Get started quickly with our template
- **Validation and error reporting**: Catch issues before import
- **Import preview**: Review before committing changes

## Quote Management

Create professional quotes and convert them to invoices seamlessly.

- Create quotes similar to invoices with the same customisation options
- Convert quotes to invoices with one click
- Quote expiration tracking
- Email delivery for quotes
- Draft quotes for work-in-progress

## Construction Industry Scheme (CIS)

Built-in UK CIS compliance for construction businesses.

- **CIS-enabled invoices**: Toggle CIS calculations per invoice
- **Deduction rates**: Support for 0%, 20%, and 30% deduction rates
- **Labour vs materials categorisation**: Properly categorise line items
- **CIS breakdown display**: Clear breakdown of deductions
- **Net payment calculations**: Automatic net payment calculations
- **Client CIS settings integration**: Store client CIS status

## Technical Excellence

Billo Invoicing is built with modern, reliable technologies:

- **Frontend:** Next.js 15, React 19, TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Backend:** tRPC, Prisma ORM
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **Authentication:** Clerk
- **Payments:** Stripe Connect
- **Email:** Resend
- **File Storage:** UploadThing

This modern stack ensures Billo Invoicing is fast, reliable, and scalable as your business grows.
